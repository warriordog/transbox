About TransFem

TransFem

TransFem is a safe space for transfem people hosted in Germany. We try our best to always provide the best user experience

Site rules

Please refrain from:

  1. Posting anything illegal.
  2. Posting pornography.
  3. No spreading of fake news or misinformation.
  4. Being a bot (unless you post unlisted and do not auto-follow).
  5. Any account setup solely to advertise a product or service.
  6. No harassment, dogpiling or doxxing of other users.
  7. No racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, or casteism

What won't get you banned:

  1. Hurting someone's feelings.
  2. Unpopular opinions voiced respectfully.
  3. Anything that can be solved by a personal block.
  4. Cursing.
  5. Lewd jokes.
  6. A bot where all posts are unlisted.
